    • The Rain Day

    by Aaisha

    The east wind comes marching over
    clouds grow dark,starts to cry
    here comes the Rain camly
    while thunder lightes above.
    flowers bloom, on the day of the Rain
    branches clash together,
    while water camly rains
    storm clouds Rumble
    on the day
    of the Rain

    Death

    by Aaisha

    Don't say you
    Don't have Death
    everyone has Death
    even the trees
    even the plants even
    the crawling ants.
    So don't you Say that
    you can live
    forever.

    When I was little

    by Aaisha

    I wonder
    when I look far away
    I saw the clouds touching the ground
    I wonder I wonder
    why can't I go there
    and touch the clouds
    I wonder I wonder
    what the clouds feel like.
